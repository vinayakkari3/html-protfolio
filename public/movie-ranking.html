My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots (2009)

"3 Idiots" is a 2009 Indian coming-of-age comedy-drama film directed by Rajkumar Hirani. The movie follows three friends, Rancho, Bittu, and Farhan, who embark on a journey to find their missing friend Rancho, who had a profound impact on their lives. The film explores themes of friendship, love, and self-discovery, and features an all-star cast, including Aamir Khan, Kareena Kapoor, and Sharman Joshi. "3 Idiots" received widespread critical acclaim and became one of the highest-grossing Indian films of all time. Reviewers praised its quirky humor, engaging storyline, and memorable performances.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara (2011)

"Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara" is a 2011 Indian drama film written and directed by Zoya Akhtar. The movie follows three friends, played by Hrithik Roshan, Farhan Akhtar, and Abhay Deol, who embark on a road trip to Spain and discover new aspects of themselves. The film received widespread critical acclaim and won several awards, including the Best Film and Best Story awards at the 2012 Filmfare Awards. Reviewers praised the film's storyline, characters, and performances, with many considering it one of the best Indian films of the 2010s.
